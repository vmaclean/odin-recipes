Buffalo Chicken Dip

How to make Buffalo Chicken Dip

A quick and easy recipe for making a chicken dip that will be a party favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Ranch
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce (Frank's Hot Sauce is personally my go to)
  • 8 oz of Cream Cheese, softened
  • 2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 cups Shredded Chicken
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, cream cheese, ranch, and buffalo sauce.
  2. Mix with a large spatula until the ingredients are fully combined.
  3. Spread the mixture in a baking dish, add a more shredded cheese on the top, and add pepper on the top layer.
  4. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees, uncovered, for about 15 to 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  5. Remove from oven and let it cool for 5 minutes. Serve with either bread, chips, or veggies of your choice. Enjoy!

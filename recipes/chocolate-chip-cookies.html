Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious chocolate chip cookies

Warm gooey chocolate chip cookie. Perfect for anytime.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened (2 stick of butter)
  • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt into a small bowl, set aside.
  3. Beat the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a large mixing bowl, mix until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time making sure they are fully mixed before adding another.
  5. Gradually beat in the flour mixture. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop or roll the cookie dough onto a cooking sheet.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes then remove the cookies and place on a wire rack to cool completely.
